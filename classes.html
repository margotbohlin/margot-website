My Favorite Classes

COMP 283 - Discrete Structures

This is probably a controversial opinion, but Discrete has been one of my favorite courses. I loved the logic questions and complexity, but most importantly I loved the people I met in the class. A lot of my closest college friends I met in office hours of Discrete!

COMP 301 - Structure/Organize Own Code

This class reaffirmed my love for computer science. I nerded out the most for this class, always completing the assignments earlier than their due date out of pure enjoyment. My favorite assignment was our final project which is linked below!

ENGL 268 - Medicine, Literature, and Culture

I took this class over the summer in 2 weeks and was scared because I have never been much of a reader, but I ended up really enjoying it and learning a lot. We talked about ethics in the health sphere and the role empathy plays into patient doctor relationships.

COMM 524 - Gender Communications and Culture

I am currently in this class, and have been enjoying it as my only humanities course this semester. We talk about gender, race, LGBTQ+ issues, class differences, and how each identity intersects. As a young queer person, this course delves into many topics that are personal to me and the friends that surround me.
